The Best Movies of All Time According to Lawrence

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

The Grand Budapest Hotel

It is a super fun movie with whimsy, where the ending doesn't really matter... it is the journey that you fall in love with.
A classic.

Samurai Cop

The best worst movie of all time.
Truly awful and truly hilarious

The Big Lebowski

Known as a stoner movie, it is a Coen Brothers' classic.
I love the strange story, characters and pacing. It is another movie where the ending is somewhat meaningless.
It is for sure the journey you will fall in love with.
Gets better with each watch!
